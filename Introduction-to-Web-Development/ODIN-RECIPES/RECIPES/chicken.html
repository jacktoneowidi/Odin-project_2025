Chicken Recipe

15-Minute Garlic Butter Chicken recipe that's perfect for a fast and flavorful meal:

ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs), sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika (or chili flakes for heat)
  • ½ tsp dried thyme (or Italian seasoning)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prep Chicken: Slice chicken into thin cutlets for faster cooking. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Cook Chicken: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. Make Sauce: In the same pan, melt butter. Add garlic, thyme, and a pinch of salt. Sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. combine: Return chicken to the pan, coat with garlic butter, and squeeze lemon juice over it. Garnish with parsley.

Serve With:

  • Rice, pasta, or a quick salad.
  • Steamed veggies or crusty bread to soak up the sauce.
