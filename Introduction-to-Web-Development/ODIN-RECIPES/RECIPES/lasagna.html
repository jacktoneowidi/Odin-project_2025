lasagna recipe

quick and easy lasagna recipe with minimal prep and no-boil noodles—ready in under 1 hour!

ingredients:

  • 9 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) tub ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding ricotta)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper to taste

instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Brown meat in a pan, drain fat, then mix in marinara sauce.
  3. Mix filling: Combine ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, egg (if using), Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer in a 9x13" dish:
    • 1st layer: Thin meat sauce → 3 noodles
    • 2nd layer: ½ ricotta mix → 3 noodles
    • 3rd layer: Remaining meat sauce → last 3 noodles → rest of ricotta → remaining mozzarella.
  5. Cover with foil, bake 25 min. Uncover, bake 10 more min until bubbly.
  6. Rest 5 min before slicing and enjoy.
