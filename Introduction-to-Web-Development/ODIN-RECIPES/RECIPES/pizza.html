pizza recipe

Dough

  • Mix 3½ cups flour + 1½ tsp salt.
  • Dissolve 2¼ tsp yeast + 1 tsp sugar in 1¼ cups warm water; wait 5 mins until bubbly.
  • Combine yeast mix + 2 tbsp olive oil with flour; knead 5 mins.
  • Let rise 1-2 hours (double in size).

Sauce

  • Sauté 2 minced garlic cloves in 1 tbsp olive oil.
  • Add 1 can crushed tomatoes + 1 tsp each (oregano, basil) + ½ tsp each (salt, pepper) + 1 tsp sugar.
  • Simmer 10 mins; cool.

    • Assemble

    • Roll dough to 12-inch circle.
    • Spread sauce, add 2 cups shredded mozzarella, then toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.).

    Bake

    • Preheat oven + pan to 475°F (245°C
    • Bake pizza 12-15 mins until crust is golden.
    • Slice & serve hot!

    For crispier crust, pre-bake dough 3 mins before topping. 🍕
